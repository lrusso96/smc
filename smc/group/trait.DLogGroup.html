This is the trait for groups where Discrete Log (DL) problem is considered to be hard.

DL problem: given a generator g of a finite group G and a random element h in G, find the integer x such that g^x = h. The two most common classes in cryptographic applications are the multiplicative group Zp* for a large prime p, and some Elliptic curve group.
